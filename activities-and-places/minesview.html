Mines Viewdeck

A stunning viewpoint showcasing sweeping vistas of mountains and valleys. It's a must-visit spot to enjoy the cool breeze and capture breathtaking scenery.

Explore the Heart of Baguio from Mines View Deck

Embark on a memorable journey to one of Baguio’s most iconic attractions, Mines View Deck. Perfect for all ages, this vantage point offers breathtaking panoramic views of the Benguet mountain ranges, lush valleys, and distant mining towns. Whether you’re visiting for stunning photos or simply to relax, Mines View Deck provides a unique perspective of Baguio’s natural beauty.

As you step onto the deck, you’ll be greeted by sweeping vistas of rolling hills, verdant forests, and the rugged terrain of the surrounding landscape. The fresh mountain air and peaceful ambiance create a perfect spot for soaking in the beauty of nature. Don’t forget to bring your camera—every angle offers a picture-perfect moment, from the serene mountain peaks to the vibrant greenery of the valleys below.

Scenic, Relaxing, and Unforgettable

The boat is equipped with comfortable seating and shaded areas, ensuring a relaxing experience for everyone on board. Feel the gentle breeze as you cruise the calm waters, and enjoy light refreshments provided during the tour. Whether you’re looking to capture incredible photos or simply unwind and take in the scenery, the Sunny Lake Boat Tour offers a peaceful escape and a new way to connect with nature.The deck is designed to provide a comfortable experience, with ample space to enjoy the view and shaded areas to relax. You can also explore nearby souvenir shops offering local crafts, snacks, and keepsakes to remember your visit. Mines View Deck is more than a viewpoint—it’s a peaceful escape that connects you with the beauty of the City of Pines.

Plan Your Visit

Mines View Deck is open daily and easily accessible from the city center, making it a must-visit spot during your Baguio adventure. To enhance your experience, consider visiting during early mornings or weekdays to avoid crowds. For more information on entrance fees, directions, and nearby attractions, check with the local tourism office or visitor center.
